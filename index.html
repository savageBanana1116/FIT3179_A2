USA Army, The strongest military force globally

Distribution of U.S Oversea military base globally - Map

After World War II, the United States had a nearly global military presence, with more than 5,000 military bases in at least 80 nations around the world (nearly half of them overseas). No other country in human history has had such a dominant presence.

These overseas military bases play an important supporting role in safeguarding US national security, expanding its global influence, improving its combat capability and protecting its core interests.

Number of active military personnel

At the beginning of the 21st century, the US military is the most powerful military institution in the world. In terms of armed forces system, it is quite different from other militaries in the world. The U.S. Armed forces are made up of active duty, reserve and civilian personnel. Currently, there are about 1.4 million active troops, all of whom are professional soldiers. The number of active duty soldiers ranks 3rd behind the world's most populous countries, China and India.

Military equipment held by each country

In the 21st century, modern combat equipment is particularly important. Modern combat forces can be divided into army, navy and air force. The map above shows the number of tanks, military aircraft, and aircraft carriers held by each country.

The United States has as many as 20 aircraft carriers, more than any other country in the world. US army also has the largest number of military aircraft, and the second largest number of tanks in the world.

Nuclear Warhead Held By Each Country

Nuclear missiles are definitely the most powerful military weapons in modern society. Especially since nearly a hundred years have passed since it was first used in World War II, the weapon has been further developed, and its power and influence have increased by an unknown number of times. As of 2022, the United States will still have 3,708 nuclear warheads. This is despite the fact that the number of nuclear warheads possessed by the United States dwindled after the Cold War.

Military spending by country

Military expenditure is the amount of money a country spends on its military. Mainly used for military construction, weapons and equipment research and development and manufacturing and war funds. Only sufficient military expenditure can make an army strong.

The United States led the ranking of countries with highest military spending in 2022, with 750 billion U.S. dollars dedicated to the military. That constituted 38 percent of the total military spending worldwide that year, which amounted to 2.1 trillion U.S. dollars
